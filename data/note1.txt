# Remote Work Policy 2026
Last Updated: January 2026

## Overview
Our company supports a "Work from Anywhere" (WFA) philosophy. This policy outlines the expectations for all full-time employees.

## Requirements
1. Employees must have a stable internet connection (minimum 50Mbps).
2. Quiet workspace for video calls is mandatory between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM EST.

## Equipment
The company provides a $1,000 yearly stipend for home office equipment, including ergonomic chairs, monitors, and peripherals. This stipend resets every January 1st.
